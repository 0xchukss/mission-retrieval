# Mission Retrieval

Mission Retrieval is a WebGL city game where Adam collects mission assets across seven symbol-based missions. Each mission begins with one main glowing marker. Enter the marker to reveal that mission's submissions, then search the city to retrieve every symbol.

## Play

Live game:

https://mission-retrieval.vercel.app

## Goal

Play as Adam and complete all seven missions by collecting every submission item.

- Mission 1: Dense Symbols
- Mission 2: Containers
- Mission 3: Floating Marks
- Mission 4: Crossbars
- Mission 5: Ghosts
- Mission 6: Angles
- Mission 7: Curves

The game tracks 94 known submissions in total. Mission items are spread around the map, including roadside corners and building edges, so use the navigation map and explore.

## Controls

Choose `PC` or `Phone` on the loading screen. Phone mode uses the desktop renderer with touch controls and starts only in landscape.

### On Foot

- `W` or Arrow Up: move forward
- `S` or Arrow Down: move backward
- `A` or Arrow Left: turn left
- `D` or Arrow Right: turn right
- `E`: enter a nearby car or hijack a moving car

Phone:

- Left and right arrows: turn
- `Run`: move Adam forward
- `E`: enter a nearby car or hijack a moving car

### In A Car

- `W`: drive forward
- `A`: steer left
- `D`: steer right
- `R`: reverse
- `S`: speed boost
- `E`: exit the car

Phone:

- Left and right arrows: steer
- `Drive`: drive forward
- `Rev`: reverse
- `Boost`: speed boost
- `Exit`: leave the car

### Game UI

- Click `Menu`: open the game menu
- Menu > `Restart`: restart the game
- Menu > `Select Mission`: jump to any mission
- Menu > `Controls`: view all controls
- Click the navigation map: expand or shrink the map

## Gameplay Tips

- Start by entering the large glowing main mission marker.
- After the marker is entered, collect the revealed submission symbols.
- A guidance prompt appears near the bottom of the screen when Adam is close to a car or inside a car.
- Cars can be entered with `E`; moving NPC cars can also be hijacked with `E`.
- Avoid crashing into walls while driving.
